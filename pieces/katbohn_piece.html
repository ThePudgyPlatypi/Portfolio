A delightful website for a voice actress out of New Jersey for not only her voice acting presence, but her personal hub for all things related to her and her work. A smattering of professionalism with a healthy dose of the color purple which personifies her personality. This was a fun project because there was creative breathing room outside of the standard professional webpage design.

This is a full Wordpress theme created from scratch featuring the show piece on the main page of a graphical equalizer in sync with the music that is playing.
