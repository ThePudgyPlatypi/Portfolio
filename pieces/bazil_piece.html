On those moonlit nights...

It always felt the same. Every time. There is an empty void inside of me that I can not fill no matter how hard I try. I know I want for nothing. My family has provided me with everything that I could ever want, and more. A life with every desire fulfilled. But yet, even still, I can not find what I need. I can not fill what haunts me on these moonlit nights.

Bazil is a character of mine that is actually no longer a woman. Things happened, and I changed my mind, but the premise behind the character is still the same. This was an image that was drawn first, and then colored over in photoshop.
