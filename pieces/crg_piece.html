Critical Response Group came to me with a challenge to redesign and completely overhaul their web presence. This Wordpress theme draws much of its inspiration from the products that Critical Response Group builds, modeling most of the pages off their Collaborative Response Graphics which feature floor plans laid on top of smart imagery with accurate grids for navigation. The design is simple and tasteful with just the right amount of animation and space to keep the site engaging.

This project was particularly challenging as I was tasked with not only building the webpage, but all the content as well. All the graphics, videos, and some of the copy is produced by yours truly. It is a daunting task, but it also helps to solidify a cohesive feel and narrative throughout the website.
