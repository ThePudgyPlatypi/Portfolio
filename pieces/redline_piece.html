Redline Motorcycles is a shop in Tabernacle specializing in Motorcycle, Dirt Bike, ATV/UTV, and Snowmobile repairs. I had a a blank canvas in terms of web presence so I built a simple webpage for them that functioned as a landing page for contact information as well as providing a short summary of what they do, and why you should choose them.

Every piece of media on the webpage, including the content, was designed and built by me. The website perfectly portrays the enthusiasm and youthful nature of the owners of the shop while keeping a air of rugged professionalism and stability.
