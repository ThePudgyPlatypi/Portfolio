Under Pressure

Curses quickly flew from her mouth as ragged harsh breaths heaved her chest. She peeked around the slowly deteriorating wall, catching a glimpse of her pursuers before a bullet devoured a chunk of the wall only inches above her head. She quickly ducked back, and huddled against the wall as a shower of AK-47 rounds pelted and popped the world around her. A few expletives later and she slowed her breathing, her eyes quickly focused, and she clutched at her rifle. It was time to punch in.
